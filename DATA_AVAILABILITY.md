# Data availability

The Cao et al. kidney stone disease GWAS summary statistics are available from Zenodo at https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.14790324. The associated GWAS Catalog study record is GCST90652506. Public transcriptomic data are available from GEO under GSE231569, GSE73680 and GSE206306. GTEx v8 / PredictDB Kidney_Cortex MASHR prediction models should be obtained from their original distribution resource and used under the applicable terms.

This repository includes author-generated result tables, figure source data, supplementary tables, manifests, selected logs and analysis code. It does not redistribute large public raw datasets, identifiable human data, LD reference data, GTEx/PredictDB model files or other third-party resources.
